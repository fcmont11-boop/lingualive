About the Developer

F
Francesco Montanile
📍 Phoenix, Arizona
Growing up in an Italian-American family, I know firsthand how difficult it can be when language stands in the way of connection. I built LinguaLive to make it easier for families and friends across the world to communicate — whether you're calling a relative in Italy, getting help from a local in another country, or simply trying to connect with someone who speaks a different language. My goal is to make real-time translation as simple and natural as a regular phone call.
